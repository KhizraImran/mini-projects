The Star of Our Kitchen

Our Star: Lotus Leaf Biryani

Imagine the moment you unwrap a fragrant parcel of lotus leaf — steam rises, the aroma of slow-cooked spices fills the air, and perfectly layered biryani rice glistens beneath. That's the Masala Magic experience.

Our Lotus Leaf Biryani is slow-cooked for hours using hand-selected basmati rice, premium mutton or chicken, and a blend of traditional spices. Wrapped in lotus leaves, the flavors infuse and deepen. Served with raita, pickles, and fresh condiments — every bite is an occasion.
