Our Story

A Place to Eat Well
& Live Better

Nestled in the heart of Pulau Tikus, No.11 Penang was born from a simple belief — that food should nourish the body and lift the spirit. Tucked along Jalan Yeoh Guan Seok, just steps from the beloved Pulau Tikus market, we've become a sanctuary for those who believe great taste and clean ingredients are never mutually exclusive.

Our kitchen is guided by one unbreakable promise: every dish is prepared with Non-GMO ingredients, free from MSG, preservatives, pork, and lard. We use Beacon Chicken — raised without antibiotics or hormones — because we believe what goes into your food matters as much as how it tastes.
