Our Story

The Art of Japanese Cuisine

At Sakura, we believe that dining is a ceremony—a moment where time slows and every detail matters. Founded on the principles of Omotenashi, the Japanese art of selfless hospitality, we bring to Kuwait an unrivaled fine-dining experience that honors tradition while embracing modern elegance.

Our master chefs—trained in Tokyo's most prestigious kitchens—craft each dish with precision and passion, using only the freshest daily ingredients. From hand-sliced sashimi to our signature wagyu creations, every bite tells a story.
