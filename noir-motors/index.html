The Atelier

Craftsmanship
Without
Compromise

In Geneva, where watchmakers have perfected the art of precision for centuries, our master craftsmen apply the same devotion to every Noir Motors vehicle. Each stitch, surface, and circuit is considered with the same deliberate attention.

I

Hand-Stitched Interiors

Every interior surface is hand-stitched by artisans trained in the traditions of haute couture. We use only the finest full-grain leather from family-owned tanneries in Tuscany.

II

Carbon Fibre Monocoque

Our chassis is laid by hand, fibre by fibre, in an autoclave process borrowed from aerospace. Each monocoque requires 340 hours of specialist labour before it ever sees an engine.

III

Dyno-Tested Perfection

Before delivery, every vehicle undergoes 72 hours of comprehensive testing — engine, transmission, chassis, aerodynamics — ensuring absolute alignment with your specification.

Technical Specification

Engineered
to Absolute
Tolerances

Every mechanical system in a Noir Motors vehicle is developed in-house by our team of 47 engineers, each a specialist in their discipline. We share nothing with the industry. We compromise nothing for the consumer.

01
Twin-Turbocharged V10 Engine
6.2-litre naturally aspirated or twin-turbo configuration, handbuilt in Geneva with a 180° flat-plane crankshaft for immediate throttle response.
02
8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission
Paddle-shift gearbox with sub-80ms gear change intervals. Rear-wheel drive as standard; optional all-wheel drive torque vectoring system.
03
Active Aerodynamics System
Adaptive front splitter and rear wing read road and speed conditions 500 times per second, generating up to 480kg of downforce at maximum velocity.
04
Carbon Ceramic Brake System
420mm front rotors with 10-piston monobloc callipers. Consistent performance from cold, fade-free from 100km/h to rest in 28 metres.
05
Adaptive Magnetorheological Suspension
Continuously variable damping responds in 3ms. Three driver modes: Comfort, Sport, and Track — each transforming the car's fundamental character.

Our Story

Born From
Obsession.
Refined By
Silence.

Noir Motors was conceived in 2019 not as a business, but as a manifesto. Our founder, a former Formula 1 aerodynamicist, gathered eleven craftsmen and one question: what would a car look like if it were built without the interference of shareholders, trend reports, or focus groups?

The answer arrived in 2021 as the first Phantom Noir — a machine so singular that the automotive world struggled to classify it. Not a supercar. Not a grand tourer. Simply: a Noir Motors.

Today we produce no more than 40 vehicles per year, each one a commissioned work. No two are identical. No two clients receive the same experience. This is the promise that defines everything we create.
