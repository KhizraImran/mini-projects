The Commission Process

From First
Conversation
to Delivery

01
Initial Consultation

Your dedicated Commissioning Director contacts you within 24 hours. A private meeting is arranged — in person at any of our showrooms, or via secure video — to understand your vision, requirements, and lifestyle.

02
Specification & Design

Over four to six months, you work with our design and engineering teams to define every aspect of your vehicle. Exterior colour, interior materials, mechanical specification, personalisation details — everything is debated until it is exactly right.

03
Construction & Progress

Your vehicle is built over approximately 2,400 hours by a dedicated team of craftsmen. You receive regular photography and video updates throughout the process. You may also arrange a visit to the atelier to observe construction in person.

04
Handover & Orientation

Your vehicle is delivered personally. A full private handover session at our Geneva atelier includes a comprehensive orientation drive, documentation of every specification decision, and a permanent relationship with your Commissioning Director for all future requirements.

All Noir Motors vehicles are built to comply with road traffic legislation in their destination market. We manage all homologation requirements as part of the commissioning process. Every Noir Motors vehicle is covered by our five-year unlimited-kilometre warranty. All servicing is conducted by our own technicians — we do not franchise service or use third-party facilities. For clients located away from our showrooms, we offer a travelling technician service. Your Commissioning Director remains your dedicated point of contact for the lifetime of your vehicle.
