The Machine
That Started
Everything.

The Phantom Noir arrived in 2021 as an answer to a question the automotive world had stopped asking: what would happen if a car was designed without compromise, without market research, and without a single concession to commercial expectation?

The result was a machine that inhabits its own category. Not supercar — too refined. Not grand tourer — too fast. Not luxury sedan — too raw. The Phantom Noir is simply, and entirely, itself.

Each one is commissioned individually. Each one differs from the last. Over the three years since its debut, no two Phantom Noirs have been identical.

Technology

Intelligence
Woven Into
Every System

Active Aerodynamics

Adaptive front splitter and rear wing generate up to 320kg of downforce. The system reads conditions 500 times per second and adjusts within 12ms.

Adaptive MR Suspension

Magnetorheological dampers respond in under 3ms, continuously adjusting to road surface data from six accelerometers and 12 body sensors.

OLED Driver Interface

A custom-designed curved OLED instrument cluster provides critical information at a glance. Content adapts to driving mode — track, sport, or touring.

Dynamic Torque Vectoring

Electronic rear differential distributes torque across both axles with 100ms precision, eliminating understeer and maximising traction at all speeds.

Meridian Signature Audio

An 18-speaker, 1,300W Meridian Signature Reference system, calibrated individually for each cabin configuration. The only audio system we approve.

Night Vision & HUD

Forward infrared camera detects obstacles at up to 280m. Key data is projected on the windscreen via full-colour HUD — speed, navigation, night-detection alerts.
