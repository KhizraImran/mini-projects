Starters

Spring Rolls & Vegetarian Spring Rolls

🍛 Mains

Mains · Signature

Chicken Kapitan Curry

⭐ Signature Dish

Rich, fragrant curry stewed slowly with lemongrass and spices. Our most-loved dish, hands down — guests return again and again just for this. A true Nyonya masterpiece passed down unchanged.

Mains

Mutton Curry

Aromatic, tender, and one of the best gravies you'll ever taste — a guest favourite. Slow-cooked until the mutton falls off the bone in a deeply spiced, warming sauce. Guests have called it life-changing.

Mains · Signature

Nasi Ulam (Basmati Rice)

⭐ Signature Dish

Exceptionally fresh and aromatic herb rice — a wonderful blend of ingredients passed down through generations. Every grain carries the fragrance of pomelo leaves, lemongrass, and torch ginger flower.

🌿 Vegan friendly
Mains · Side

Sambal Okra

Top-notch and full of flavour — a beloved side dish that disappears from the table first. The sambal paste is made from scratch, giving it a deep, rounded heat that complements every main.

Mains · Seafood

Stir-Fried Squid

Perfectly cooked, full of wok flavour — the squid is tender with a satisfying bite, tossed with aromatics and seasoned to the point of perfection. Simple, but everything is in the technique.

Mains · Vegetable

Stir-Fried Eggplant

A guest-favourite side — simple, humble, and delicious. The eggplant soaks up every bit of flavour from the wok, becoming silky and deeply savoury. A perfect example of Kampung Style cooking at its best.

🌿 Vegan
Mains · Kampung Style

Kiam Hu Kut Gulai

Salted Fish Bone Stew

A true Kampung Style dish with strong Peranakan, Hokkien and Malay roots — humble, thrifty, and deeply flavourful. This is the kind of dish you can't find anywhere else. The kind that makes you feel like you're sitting in someone's grandmother's kitchen.

🍜 Rice & Noodles

Rice & Noodles

Nyonya Udon

Fragrant noodles seasoned with pomelo leaves, lemongrass and shrimp paste — slightly spicy, deeply layered. A dish that carries the full complexity of Nyonya cooking in every mouthful.

Rice & Noodles

Duck Slices with Egg Ramen Noodles

Comforting broth with tender duck slices — a lunchtime favourite. The broth is simmered low and slow, creating a depth of flavour that warms you from the inside out. Served with springy egg noodles.

Rice & Noodles · Simple

Onion Omelette

Simple, humble, and always satisfying. A reminder that the best comfort food needs no introduction — just good eggs, sweet onions, and a well-seasoned pan. Perfect paired with steamed rice.

🍮 Desserts & Drinks

Desserts

Bubur Cha Cha

A comforting coconut milk dessert with sweet yam and taro — the perfect finish to a Nyonya meal. Warm or chilled, it's a bowl of pure comfort that connects you to generations of Peranakan tradition.

Desserts

Barley with Beancurd & Gingko Dessert

A gentle, comforting classic Peranakan sweet. The barley is cooked until soft and creamy, the beancurd silken, and the gingko nuts add a subtle earthy warmth. Light enough to enjoy at the end of a hearty meal.

Drinks

Iced Nutmeg Juice

Refreshing, sweet, with a mild spiced flavour — a true local Penang favourite. Made from fresh nutmeg, this drink is rare outside of Penang and a must-try for every visitor. Cooling, fragrant, unforgettable.
