Our Heritage

Born from a
Grandmother's Kitchen

Niang Penang was born from the legacy of Nyonya cooking — a centuries-old culinary tradition blending Chinese and Malay flavors. Every dish on our menu is an heirloom recipe, perfected over generations, seasoned with love and the spices of Penang's rich cultural soul.

We cook the way grandmothers always have — with patience, with precision, and with an unwavering belief that food is the most powerful form of storytelling. Come, sit at our table, and taste a legacy.

Signature Flavours

The Niang Table

Flavors that have stood the test of time

Nyonya Curry Laksa

A soul-warming bowl of coconut-rich curry broth, silken tofu, prawns, and rice vermicelli. Our laksa recipe has been simmering since 1962.

RM 22

Assam Pedas Siakap

Barramundi in a tangy tamarind and galangal broth, spiked with bird's eye chillies, okra, and wild ginger. Bold, fiery, unforgettable.

RM 38

Nasi Ulam Peranakan

Fragrant herb rice tossed with toasted coconut, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaf, and bunga kantan — served with traditional Nyonya sides.

RM 26

Kuih Talam Pandan

Layered steamed Nyonya cake with a creamy pandan-infused base and a velvety coconut milk top layer. The grandmother's sweetest memory.
