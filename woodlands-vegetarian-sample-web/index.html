Our Heritage

Decades of Pure, Honest Flavour

Rooted in the heart of Little India, George Town — a UNESCO World Heritage enclave — Woodlands has been serving pure vegetarian Indian cuisine for decades. From the first paper dosa at 8:30 in the morning to a hearty thali at noon, every dish is made with the same commitment to authentic South and North Indian cooking. No meat. No compromise.
