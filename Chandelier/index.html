Our Story

Two Decades of Unforgettable Evenings

Since 2005, Chandelier has been Seputeh's most beloved destination for elevated dining, vibrant nightlife, and landmark celebrations.

Est. 2005 — Seputeh, KL

Born from a Dream of Grandeur

What began as an intimate fine-dining concept in the heart of Seputeh has grown into one of Kuala Lumpur's most celebrated multi-experience venues. From the very first evening, Chandelier was designed around one belief: that great food deserves a great atmosphere.

Over twenty years, we've perfected the balance between warmth and sophistication — a space where business dinners, romantic evenings, and wild celebrations all feel equally at home.

The Bar & Lounge

Sip, Unwind, Stay a While

Our bar is a destination in itself. Hand-crafted cocktails, an extensive wine cellar, premium spirits from around the world — all served in an atmosphere that knows how to slow time down.

Whether you're starting the evening with aperitifs or lingering late with nightcaps, our bartenders are artists in their own right, curating each drink with the same care as our kitchen.

Live Music & Entertainment

Where Music Sets the Mood

Every weekend, our stage comes alive with live jazz bands, soulful vocalists, and electric ensembles. The music at Chandelier isn't background noise — it's part of the dining experience.

Our private karaoke rooms offer a more personal stage for groups who want to take the spotlight themselves, fully equipped and acoustically designed for maximum fun.
