Our Story

A Legacy of
Malaysian Elegance

Nestled in the cultural heart of Penang, Gala House reimagines the island's rich culinary heritage through a lens of modern luxury.

Every plate tells a story — from hand-ground rempah passed down through generations to meticulously sourced seafood from the Straits of Malacca. Our kitchen bridges tradition and innovation, creating an experience that honours Malaysia's diverse flavours while elevating them to haute cuisine standards.

Whether you're celebrating a milestone or simply seeking an extraordinary evening, Gala House invites you to dine slowly, savour deeply, and leave transformed.
