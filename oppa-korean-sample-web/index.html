Our Story

Born from Passion,
Fired by Flavour

OPPA Korean BBQ began as a beloved neighbourhood spot before finding its permanent home at 139 Lebuh Carnarvon, George Town — right on the vibrant Armenian Street Heritage corridor in the heart of Penang. What started as a humble Korean eatery quickly became one of the island's most talked-about dining experiences.

Managed with a passion for authentic Korean flavours, every dish at OPPA is crafted to honour the traditions of Korean cuisine — from perfectly marinated Samgyeopsal sizzling on the tabletop grill, to deeply comforting bowls of Kimchi Jjigae. The banchan are made fresh daily, and the all-you-can-eat buffet experience is designed for those who believe great food deserves no limits.

The Experience

Build Your
Perfect Grill

Every visit is an experience crafted around your table. Here's how your OPPA BBQ journey unfolds.

01
Choose Your Buffet or À La Carte
Select the all-you-can-eat buffet (from RM36/pax, min. 2 persons) or order individual items from our authentic Korean menu.
02
Premium Banchan Arrives First
A spread of complimentary side dishes hits the table — kimchi, egg rolls, stir-fried potato strips, and seasonal banchan.
03
Grill, Wrap & Savour
Our staff assist with the grill whenever needed, changing the BBQ plate and ensuring every cut is cooked to golden perfection.
04
Wrap It in Ssam & Enjoy
Take a crisp lettuce leaf, add grilled meat, kimchi, and house ssamjang — wrap and savour the full Korean BBQ ritual.
