Our Story

A Kitchen Born from
Kampung Roots

From Mak's wooden stove to our beloved restaurant in Cybersouth — every grain of rice carries the soul of home cooking.

The Beginning

More Than a Restaurant — A Family Legacy

Zaleena Nasi Kukus was born from a mother's desire to share the food she grew up making in her kampung — recipes passed down through generations, never written, only felt. Every dish is a memory, every spice a story.

Located at Park Place 1@Cybersouth, our restaurant has become a cornerstone of the Dengkil community. Families drive from Putrajaya, Kajang, and Kuala Lumpur for a plate of our signature Nasi Kukus — fragrant steamed rice that fills not just the stomach, but the soul.

We believe in cooking slowly, with care. Our ayam is marinated overnight. Our rendang simmers for hours. Our sambal is ground fresh each morning. There are no shortcuts to authentic taste.

With a 4.8-star rating earned purely through the love of our customers, we remain humble — grateful for every diner who sits at our table and every order that travels back to your home.
