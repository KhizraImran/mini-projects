Our Story

A Legacy Rooted
in Culinary Art

Founded in 1988 by Master Chef Cheang Wei Liang, our restaurant has been the heartbeat of authentic Chinese gastronomy in Penang for over three decades. Every dish is a tribute to the rich, layered traditions of Cantonese and Sichuan cooking.

We source only the finest seasonal ingredients — from hand-picked herbs along the slopes of Cameron Highlands to day-fresh seafood from the Straits of Malacca — crafting a dining journey that honours the soul of Chinese culinary philosophy.

Meet the Maestro

Master Chef
Cheang Wei Liang

With over 35 years commanding the wok, Chef Cheang is one of Malaysia's most celebrated Chinese culinary masters. Trained in Guangzhou, Hong Kong, and Chengdu, he brings an unparalleled breadth of technique — from the delicate fold of a har gow to the fierce heat of a Cantonese claypot.
