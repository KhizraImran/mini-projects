Menu Concepts

Nestled along the charming Lorong Aboo Sittee in historic George Town, Penang, Nanya Western Kitchen (南雅西厨) was born from a simple belief — that great food, prepared with care, can bring people together.

We offer a unique dual-concept dining experience: by day, enjoy beloved local flavours like our signature Stir-Fried Laksa and creamy Aromatic Curry Prawn Nasi Lemak; by evening, savour hearty Western classics — chicken chops, loaded fries, and comfort food — crafted fresh in our kitchen.

With a 4.9/5 Google rating from over 200 happy diners, we're proud to be one of George Town's most loved neighbourhood restaurants. Our air-conditioned space offers a welcome retreat from Penang's tropical warmth, whether you're stopping in for a solo lunch or gathering with family.

Can't make it in? We're also available on FoodPanda for delivery right to your door across George Town.
