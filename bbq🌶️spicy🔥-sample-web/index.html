Our Story

Where Tradition Meets the Flame

Born from a deep love for Arabic culinary heritage, BBQ Spicy Restaurant Farwaniya is more than a place to eat — it's an experience. Every cut of meat is marinated with hand-selected spices, grilled over live charcoal, and served with warmth and pride.

Located in the vibrant heart of Farwaniya, Kuwait, we invite families, friends, and food lovers to gather around the fire and create memories that last.
