The Story of MAMU

In Malay and Tamil, 'Mamu' is what you call the uncle behind the counter — the one who knows exactly how much curry you like, who remembers your order before you say it. At MAMU Heritage, that spirit lives on in every plate. We bring you the honest, hearty flavours of Malaysian mamak cooking — unchanged, uncompromised.
