How To Find Us

We are located at No. 95-2, Jalan Rakyat in Brickfields — KL's vibrant Little India neighbourhood. The easiest way to reach us is via KL Sentral station (all LRT, KTM, MRT, ERL lines), just a short walk away. Ample street parking is also available in the surrounding area.


Brickfields is one of the most accessible neighbourhoods in Kuala Lumpur. With KL Sentral as the central transit hub, you can reach us from anywhere in the Klang Valley within minutes. Whether you're travelling in for dinner or catching a late-night bite between train connections, MAMU Heritage is always open and always ready.
