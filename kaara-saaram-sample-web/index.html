Our Heritage

Spice Runs in Our Veins

Kaara Saaram was born in the heart of Brickfields — KL's Little India — where the aroma of spices fills every lane. Our founders grew up watching grandmothers stir claypots over wood fires, and carried those memories into every recipe we serve today.

From a single branch in Brickfields to 15+ locations across Malaysia, we've never lost what made us great: honest ingredients, ancient techniques, and the warmth of community.
