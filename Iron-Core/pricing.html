FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining the IronCore family.

Is there a joining or enrollment fee?
No — IronCore has zero enrollment or setup fees. You pay only your chosen monthly membership. We believe in transparent, honest pricing with nothing hidden. The price you see is the price you pay from day one.
Can I pause or cancel my membership anytime?
Absolutely. IronCore operates on a no-contract, month-to-month model. You can pause your membership for up to 3 months (great for travel or injury) or cancel anytime with just 7 days notice before your next billing date. No cancellation fees — ever.
Do I need to be fit already to join IronCore?
Absolutely not. IronCore welcomes athletes of every level — from complete beginners to seasoned competitors. All of our trainers specialize in meeting you exactly where you are and building from there. Every journey starts with a free fitness assessment on your first visit.
What's included in the Personal Training sessions?
Personal Training sessions are 60 minutes, one-on-one with a certified elite coach of your choice. Sessions include technique coaching, personalized programming, performance tracking, and a debrief with notes emailed to you after each session. Pro members get 2 sessions per month; Elite members enjoy unlimited sessions.
Are there family or corporate membership discounts?
Yes! We offer 15% off for family add-ons (spouse/partner/dependent) and custom corporate wellness packages for teams of 5 or more. Contact us directly at hello@ironcorefitness.com or fill out the form on our Contact page and our team will craft a package for you within 24 hours.
