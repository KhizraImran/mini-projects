Our Story

Built on Iron,
Driven by Fire

IronCore Fitness was founded in 2009 by Marcus Kane — former Army Ranger turned strength coach — in a 3,000 sq ft warehouse with 12 barbells and a vision. That vision was simple: build a gym where serious athletes could train without excuses.

Today, IronCore spans 28,000 sq ft of elite training space across 3 locations, serves 5,200+ active members, and employs 48 of the world's most certified coaches. We didn't water it down to appeal to everyone — we doubled down on intensity, expertise, and results.

2009

IronCore is Born

Marcus Kane opens the original 3,000 sq ft warehouse location in Downtown Manhattan with 12 barbells and an unshakeable mission.

2012

First Expansion — Brooklyn

Explosive member growth drives opening of second location in Brooklyn, doubling capacity and adding full CrossFit rig and boxing area.

2015

IronCore Pro Program Launched

Launch of the flagship Pro membership tier with unlimited classes and dedicated nutrition coaching. 2,000 members milestone reached.

2018

Third Location — Queens

New 28,000 sq ft flagship facility opens with state-of-the-art recovery zone, spa, and full indoor track.

2021

IronCore App Launched

Digital coaching platform rolls out with workout tracking, nutrition logging, and direct coach messaging — 5,000 downloads in first month.

2024

5,200+ Members & Growing

IronCore celebrates 15 years of excellence with 5,200 active members, 48 coaches, and plans for 2 new locations launching in 2025.
