Our Story

Where French Tradition Meets Penang Soul

Nestled in the heart of George Town's heritage quarter, Lumiere X Dejeuner Bistro reimagines classic French gastronomy through the lens of Southeast Asian terroir. Our open kitchen invites you into a theatre of culinary precision, where every plate tells a story of provenance and passion.

Founded by a collective of French-trained chefs with deep roots in Penang's vibrant food culture, our bistro bridges two worlds -- the meticulous discipline of Parisian kitchens and the bold, aromatic spirit of Malaysian ingredients.
