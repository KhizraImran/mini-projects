OUR STORY

Born from Passion. Crafted with Purpose.

Est. 2024 · George Town, Penang

Born from Passion,
Crafted with Purpose

Siam Social Penang was born from Calvin Lim's vision to bring something genuinely different to George Town's vibrant food scene. A former banker with a passion for design and cuisine, Calvin transformed 73 Noordin Street into one of Penang's most distinctive dining destinations.

The space reflects his eye for detail — high white timber ceilings, earthy tones, curated artwork and lush greenery create a calm yet vibrant atmosphere. A tranquil pool on the ground floor and a vinyl records tapas bar upstairs make Siam Social a truly multi-layered experience.

What makes our food authentic is its connection to Northern Thailand. Calvin's brother runs 1000 Stars — a boutique glamping retreat in Chiang Rai's mountains — who sent his chef to Penang to train our team in the traditions of Northern Thai cuisine.

From bold Pad Kaprao to our signature Thai Grilled Fish and the beloved Chilli Padi Pizza, every dish is crafted with care — and a menu that bridges Northern Thailand and the global palate.

"Siam Social offers a culinary journey through the vibrant flavors of Thai cuisine — a favourite among locals and visitors alike."
