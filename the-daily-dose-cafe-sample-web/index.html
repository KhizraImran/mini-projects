SINCE THE FIRST POUR

A little slower,
a little warmer.

The Daily Dose Cafe began as a single espresso machine and a stubborn belief: mornings deserve more care. Tucked into a quiet Penang street, we roast in small batches, bake before sunrise, and set tables the way we'd want to find them ourselves — unhurried.

Every cup is pulled to order. Every pastry is made that day. No shortcuts, just good habits kept daily.
