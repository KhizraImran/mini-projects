The Siuyuk Story

Siuyuk — crispy crackling roast pork — is the heartbeat of Aunty Q. Tony spent years perfecting the marinade, the temperature, the crackling. What started as a home kitchen experiment in 2017 became Penang's most creative roast pork experience.

We put Siuyuk in noodles, rice, sushi, burgers, steamed buns, and even cake. Because great pork deserves great company.

"At first, the meat texture was like chewing gum. We kept trying until we got it right." — Tony, Founder

A Family Affair, A Penang Legacy

Tony and Jane built Aunty Q from the ground up — starting with a dream, a centralized kitchen, and a set of treasured family recipes. What began as an online roast pork business blossomed into four beloved outlets across Penang, each serving families daily with the same warmth and passion.

Every recipe carries a memory — Tony's mother's hand-marinated pork, Jane's father's secret spice blend. The flavours are Penang's own, passed down with love and served with pride.
