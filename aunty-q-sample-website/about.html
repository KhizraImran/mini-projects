Our Story

From a home kitchen to Penang's heart.

The Beginning

It Started with Roast Pork

In 2017, Tony and Jane started from their home kitchen with one obsession — making the perfect Siuyuk (crispy roast pork). The marinade, the temperature, the crackling — they tried and failed and tried again.

"At first the meat texture was like chewing gum," Tony recalls. "But we kept going." Their relentless efforts paid off.

By 2018, the first Aunty Q Kitchen & Bar opened at Summerton, Bayan Lepas — and Penang's love affair with Aunty Q began. What started as a simple online roast pork business blossomed into a full restaurant experience, serving not just Siuyuk but an entire menu of heritage-inspired and fusion dishes that quickly won the hearts of Penangites and visitors alike.

The Heart of Our Kitchen

The Recipes Behind the Brand

Every dish at Aunty Q carries a story. The recipes come from Tony's mother and Jane's father — heritage flavours of a Penang childhood. Our founders took these treasured family recipes and reimagined them: Siuyuk in sushi, in burgers, in noodles, in cake.

Traditional meets creative. Comfort meets innovation. Every plate is crafted with the same care and love that Tony's mother and Jane's father poured into their family meals — now shared with all of Penang.

The name "Aunty Q" is a nod to the warmth and familiarity of family — that comforting figure who always had something delicious cooking on the stove. It's the feeling we want every guest to carry home.

"We cook the way your mother would — with every ingredient chosen with love, and every dish made to feel like home."

— Tony & Jane, Founders of Aunty Q

Our Journey

From Kitchen to Community

Every year brought us closer to becoming a true Penang institution.

2017

Home kitchen. Online roast pork business begins. Tony and Jane perfect their signature Siuyuk marinade and crackling technique.

2018

First outlet opens: Aunty Q Kitchen & Bar, Summerton, Bayan Lepas. Penang's love affair with Aunty Q begins.

2021

Aunty Q Noodles opens at Arena Curve, Bayan Baru — bringing the beloved menu to a new Penang neighbourhood.

2024

Expansion continues: Island 88 Mall (Tanjung Tokong) & Juru Sentral Jaya (Bukit Mertajam) open, bringing Aunty Q to both islands and the mainland.

Today

4 branches, beloved Penang heritage brand — serving families daily with the same warmth, creativity, and roast pork perfection that started it all.

The People Behind the Plates

Meet the Founders

Tony & Jane built Aunty Q from scratch — with family recipes, big dreams, and an unshakeable belief that great food should feel like home. Their story is one of perseverance, creativity, and an unwavering love for Penang's rich culinary heritage.


Together, they combined Tony's mother's roast pork mastery with Jane's father's spice wisdom — and created something entirely new and deeply familiar at the same time. Today, that spirit lives in every dish served across all four Aunty Q outlets.
